Privacy Policy

At RL Shine, we are committed to protecting your personal information and maintaining your trust. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or purchase our products.

b. Order & Transaction Information: Product details, Payment status (Note: We do not store your card or banking details)

c. Technical Information: IP address, Device type, Browser details, Website usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: Process and deliver your orders, Provide customer support, Communicate order updates, Improve our products and services, Prevent fraudulent transactions. We do not use your data for unnecessary or intrusive purposes.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information only with: Delivery partners (for shipping your order), Payment service providers (for secure transactions), Authorities if required by law.

4. Data Security

At RL Shine, we take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or alteration.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page.

Refund & Return Policy

Due to the nature of our cleaning and hygiene products, RL Shine follows a strict return and refund policy.

1. Eligible Returns

Returns are accepted only if: Product is damaged, defective, or leaking, or Incorrect product is delivered.

2. Conditions for Return

Request must be raised within 3 days of delivery, Product must be unused and in original packaging, Photo or video proof is required.

3. Non-Returnable Cases

We do not accept returns for: Used products, Change of mind, Minor packaging damage, Improper handling or misuse.

4. Inspection & Approval

All return requests are subject to inspection. RL Shine reserves the right to approve or reject any request.

5. Refund Process

Refunds are processed within 5–7 working days after approval. Amount will be credited to the original payment method.
